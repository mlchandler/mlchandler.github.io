Twitter Posters

Twitter posters were brought to my attention by Mike Morrison and were conceived as an alternative to conferences which were postponed and cancelled as a result of the covid-19 pandemic. These Twitter Posters are intended to be a relatively low effort (after all you have already done the hard work of conducting your research!) way of communicating science to a wider group of people using GIFs to present a series of simple slides on twitter. For more information about Twitter Posters see Mike's youtube video here, where he also provides templates to create your own!

I have taken to making Twitter Posters for my peer-reviewed research as a way of quickly and simply communicating the key findings from my work to a broad audience. These Twitter Posters are all available on my twitter. With direct links to each Twitter Poster below [or imbed the twitter posters?].
