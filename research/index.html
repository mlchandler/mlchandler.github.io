Fiordland Current

The Fiordland Current is a poleward-flowing EBC along the Fiordland coast of southwest New Zealand. Satellite altimetry was used to estimate the mean, interannual variability, decadal variability, and longer-term trends in the across-track surface geostrophic velocity of the current. Possible drivers of trends and variability were identified. A paper has been published on this work.

This work was supervised by Melissa Bowen and Robert Smith.
